W or UP		:	accelerate forward
S or DOWN	:	brake/accelerate backward
A or LEFT 	:	rotate left
D or RIGHT 	:	rotate right
SPACE 		:	handbrake
ENTER		:	pause/resume
MOUSE MOVE/DRAG	:	aim	
MOUSE KEY LEFT	:	fire primary weapon (hold to auto fire)
MOUSE KEY RIGHT	:	fire secundary weapon (hold to auto fire)
